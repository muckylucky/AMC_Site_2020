TL:DR

Very marketer friendly with plenty of features aimed at lowering the learning curve and providing ease-of-entry including reports sent directly to the marketers inbox.

Throughout Emarsys’ website they repeatedly refer to the insights they have gained across +1,500 of their customers to produce “Impact ready” solutions across their feature base.
