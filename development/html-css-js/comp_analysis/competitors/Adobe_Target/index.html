TL:DR

Adobe Target is the Adobe Experience Cloud solution that provides everything you need to tailor and personalize your customers’ experiences. Target helps you maximize revenue on your web and mobile sites, apps, social media, and other digital channels.

Of all the competitors analysed so far Adobe’s Target bears the most similarities with Site. As a module of Adobe’s Experience Cloud it’s focus is online (website) personalisation and testing by leveraging AI to produce individually tailored Experiences. These Experiences comprise of Recommendations, Experiences, Locations and Offers (sound familiar?)

Like other offerings it also has a vast wealth of templated solutions to get new users started - referred to as recipes.

The focus of Target would appear to be ease-of use out-of-the-box and AI powered automation - freeing marketers to focus on their strategy and not their tactics.

With Sensei Adobe leverages the powerul AI driven features from their CC suite products in a marketer friendly package.
