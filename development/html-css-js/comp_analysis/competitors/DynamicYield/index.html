TL:DR

Dynamic Yield is the market leader. Its platform is focused on offering robust digital commerce personalization capabilities. It appears to have an easy learning curve with templates and predictive audiences easing some of the pain into creating campaigns/content and segmentation.

Visually it has a nice looking interface free from clutter that doesn’t overwhelm the user with options. Product Demo. The site personalization workflow looks similar to Site, broken down into Site Peronalisation > Content (Action) > Create ‘Experience’ (Offer). Users also have access to an online library of Use-Case based templates.

Notably it it’s support for marketing (i.e. batch) personalisation is weak in comparison to SMC.
